Privacy Policy

TextForge — Local AI Writing Assistant
Effective date: May 24, 2026

TextForge does not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on any external server. Your text never leaves your machine.

What TextForge does

TextForge is a Chrome extension that rewrites, summarizes, and edits text in your browser's text fields. It sends your text only to a local AI server running on your own computer (such as Ollama or LM Studio) via an endpoint URL that you configure. No third party ever receives your text.

Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect the text you write or edit.
  • We do not collect any personally identifiable information.
  • We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or tracking of any kind.
  • We do not transmit data to any server we operate.

What is stored locally

TextForge stores the following settings using chrome.storage.sync:

  • Endpoint URL — the address of your local AI server (e.g. http://localhost:11434)
  • Model name — the AI model you selected
  • API key — optional bearer token for authenticated local endpoints

These settings are synced across your own Chrome profiles via Google's built-in Chrome Sync infrastructure. They are never sent to us or any third party beyond Google's standard Chrome Sync service, which is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Network access

The extension requests broad network permissions (http://*/* and https://*/*) solely to allow the content script to fetch from your user-configured AI endpoint, which can be any IP address on your local network (e.g. a second machine running Ollama). The extension makes no outbound requests other than to the endpoint URL you explicitly set in the settings popup.

Permissions explained

  • storage — saves your endpoint, model, and API key settings locally
  • activeTab — injects the floating icon into the page you are actively viewing
  • host_permissions (all URLs) — allows the content script to call your configured localhost or local-network AI endpoint from any page

Children's privacy

TextForge is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.
